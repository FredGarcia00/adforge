# AdForge - Mission & Goals

## Mission Statement

AdForge is an AI-powered TikTok UGC (User-Generated Content) video generator that enables creators, brands, and agencies to create viral content at scale. Our goal is to democratize professional video content creation by making it accessible, fast, and affordable.

---

## Core Value Proposition

**"Create viral TikTok videos in seconds, not hours."**

- Turn product descriptions into engaging UGC-style videos
- No video editing skills required
- Scale content production without scaling costs
- Focus on strategy while AI handles creation

---

## Target Users

1. **Solo Creators** - Building personal brands, affiliate marketing
2. **E-commerce Brands** - Product promotions, UGC-style ads
3. **Marketing Agencies** - Managing multiple client accounts
4. **Dropshippers** - High-volume product content needs

## Competitive Advantages vs Reel.Farm

1. **Avatar-based videos** - Not just slideshows, actual AI presenters
2. **Studio-style UI** - Familiar, professional interface like YouTube Studio
3. **Bulk management** - Select and manage multiple videos at once
4. **Better pricing** - More competitive at entry level
5. **Modern tech stack** - Next.js 15, better performance

## Success Metrics

### User Engagement
- Daily Active Users (DAU)
- Videos generated per user per month
- User retention (30-day, 90-day)

### Business
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Conversion rate (Free to Paid)
- Churn rate

### Product
- Video generation success rate
- Average generation time
- User satisfaction (NPS)

---

## Monetization Strategy

1. **Freemium Model** - Free tier with limited videos to acquire users
2. **Usage-Based Upsell** - Users naturally upgrade when hitting limits
3. **Feature Gates** - Advanced features (scheduling, multi-account) in paid tiers
4. **Agency Focus** - Higher-value customers with unlimited needs
